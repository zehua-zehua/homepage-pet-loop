# Loopi v0.2 Evolution Report

Date: 2026-06-15
Version: `loopi_v0_2`
Decision: promote to active homepage version

## Trigger

Manual product feedback indicated that `loopi_v0_1` felt too animal-like. The desired direction is a stronger virtual avatar / AI companion identity, while optionally borrowing ENFP-like dog warmth from the provided reference image.

## Diagnosis

`loopi_v0_1` validated the minimal feedback system and the horse-origin concept, but the full four-legged pony body made the character read too much like a pet illustration. That weakens the personal-brand role because visitors may focus on the animal species instead of the AI companion system.

## Design Move

`loopi_v0_2` changes Loopi into a semi-upright virtual companion avatar. The horse source is retained through swept blue-purple hair, motion energy, and the tail-like plume. Dog-like cues are limited to soft ears, open expression, and ENFP warmth. The character keeps cyan AI marks, a smart collar, and the silver-white body.

## Expected Improvement

- Stronger virtual avatar reading.
- Less literal animal anatomy.
- More approachable ENFP-like first impression.
- Better fit with a personal AI product homepage.

## Homepage Feedback Model

The `Loopi 首页反馈问卷 v1.0` now lives in Pet Lab. The homepage keeps only the Loopi display, compact feedback status, and a Pet Lab entry point so the long questionnaire does not disrupt the main personal-brand page.

The form evaluates Loopi as a homepage AI companion, not as a generic mascot preference check.

The survey has 8 questions scored from 1 to 5:

- A. Visual first impression: overall beauty and professional visual quality.
- B. Homepage fit: whether Loopi feels natural on `yuanzehua.me` and strengthens the owner impression instead of distracting.
- C. Personal temperament fit: whether Loopi conveys curiosity, AI exploration, friendliness, and approachability.
- D. Pony + puppy concept: whether Loopi conveys pony-like momentum/growth and puppy-like warmth/companionship.

The database still stores one row per feedback event. The public `score` is the rounded average of the 8 questions, and per-question scores are saved as structured tags.

## Risks To Watch

- Could still feel too cute if eyes or smile dominate.
- Could drift into a generic puppy if dog-like cues increase.
- Could lose the zodiac-horse memory point if the motion and hair language are reduced too far.
- Could score well as a cute image while failing the homepage role; the feedback model now treats that as a risk, not a win.

## Next Candidate Variables

- Avatar maturity.
- ENFP warmth intensity.
- AI product cue strength.

## Decision

Use `loopi_v0_2` as the active homepage version and collect feedback under `loopi_v0_2`. Keep `loopi_v0_1` as archived history.
