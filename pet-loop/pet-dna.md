# Loopi Pet DNA v1.1

Loopi is the evolving virtual AI companion of `yuanzehua.me`.

Loopi is not a decorative pet icon and is no longer constrained to a literal pony body. It is a small personal-brand experiment: a premium 3D companion avatar whose origin comes from Zehua Yuan's zodiac sign, the horse. The horse source should express momentum, curiosity, growth, and continuous iteration, while the visible character should read first as a virtual companion for an AI-native personal homepage.

## Role

- Name: Loopi
- Current version: `loopi_v0_2`
- Identity: pony-origin virtual AI companion avatar for a personal AI product homepage
- Function: lightweight interaction entry, feedback object, and visible Loop Engineering artifact
- Placement: secondary to Zehua's own identity; Loopi must not replace the personal introduction, work, writing, or contact sections.

## Personality

- Smart
- Warm
- Curious
- Focused
- Reliable
- ENFP-like openness and optimistic energy
- Young but not childish
- Friendly but not overly cute
- AI-native but not cold

## Visual DNA

Loopi should read as a premium, clean, friendly 3D virtual companion for a professional personal website.

Required traits:

- Virtual companion avatar first; animal mascot second.
- Subtle pony-origin cues through momentum, swept hair, tail-like energy, or compact stance, not realistic horse anatomy.
- Optional dog-like ENFP warmth through soft floppy-ear influence, open expression, and loyal companion feeling.
- Silver-white or light-gray body.
- Blue-purple gradient hair tuft and tail-like energy plume.
- Warm cyan-blue eye highlight.
- Minimal smart collar, chest light, forehead ring, or other subtle AI companion detail.
- A few restrained data-particle cues only when they do not look like game magic.
- Soft futuristic companion feeling, polished 3D material, gentle lighting.

## Palette

- Body silver white: `#F2F5F7`
- Website paper background: `#F5F2ED`
- Light gray shadow: `#C9D1D9`
- Blue gradient: `#8EA7FF`
- Purple gradient: `#9B7CFF`
- Cyan glow: `#5EEAD4`
- Detail gray: `#374151`

## Hard Avoids

- Literal four-legged pony, realistic horse anatomy, visible hooves, saddle, reins, or riding cues.
- Pure dog, generic puppy mascot, cat, fox, dragon, or generic robot.
- My Little Pony style, children's cartoon style, toy-like plastic, NFT avatar style.
- Cyberpunk robot horse, heavy mecha, aggressive armor, fantasy mount.
- Oversized eyes, rainbow mane, excessive neon, game effects, magic effects.
- Any version that weakens professional credibility or makes the visitor unsure who Zehua is.

## Iteration Rules

- Each candidate may change only 1-2 variables.
- Stable variables: virtual AI companion identity, pony-origin momentum, silver-white body, blue-purple hair/energy, warm intelligent eyes, professional homepage fit.
- Candidate variables: expression, ear shape, stance, hair flow, tail-energy shape, glow intensity, collar detail, material finish, body proportion, professional-feeling strength.
- A candidate cannot go to homepage production unless it improves or preserves professional trust, brand fit, AI-native feeling, and homepage fit.
- Dog-like cues must support warmth and openness; they must not turn Loopi into a generic puppy.

## Evaluation Dimensions

The homepage feedback survey is `loopi_homepage_feedback_v1`.

Rating scale:

- 1 = very disagree.
- 3 = neutral.
- 5 = very agree.

The public `score` is the rounded average of eight questions.

Dimensions and questions:

- Visual first impression:
  - Loopi looks good overall.
  - Loopi's visual quality fits a professional personal homepage.
- Homepage fit:
  - Loopi does not feel abrupt on `yuanzehua.me`.
  - Loopi strengthens the first impression of the site owner instead of distracting from it.
- Personal fit:
  - Loopi makes the site owner feel curious and willing to explore AI.
  - Loopi makes the site owner feel friendly and easy to talk to.
- Pony + dog concept:
  - Loopi communicates pony-like momentum, growth, and forward motion.
  - Loopi communicates dog-like warmth, happiness, and companionship.

Red lines:

- Visual first impression average below 3.5.
- Homepage fit average below 3.5.
- Personal fit average below 3.5.
- Pony + dog concept average below 3.3.
- Question 4, first impression without distraction, below 3.5.
- Question 7 or 8 repeatedly below 3.3, indicating the hybrid character concept is not readable.
- Pet DNA drift.
